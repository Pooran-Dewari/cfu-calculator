CFU Calculator

This tool calculates colony-forming units per milliliter (cfu/mL) and determines the volume of bacterial culture required to achieve a target CFU (e.g., for a bath challenge).

  • To calculate CFU/mL at OD = 1.0, input OD of the original culture used and volume spread for colony counting (µL).
  • To calculate required culture volume to reach target CFU (default 1.0E+06), input the OD600 of your bacterial culture.
